About Me

Hello! I’m Adewole Salimot, the creative mind behind IdeaPlus Graphix. With over 4 years of experience in the graphic design industry, I specialize in flyer design, image tracing, and document layout. My passion for digital art began with curiosity and turned into a career driven by precision, innovation, and 100% client satisfaction.

Throughout my design journey, I’ve mastered tools like CorelDRAW, Photoshop, and Microsoft Word, and I’m always exploring new styles to make my designs stand out. IdeaPlus Graphix is not just a brand — it’s a promise of excellence and creativity in every design delivered.
